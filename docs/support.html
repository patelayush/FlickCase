Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some common questions users have about FlickCase. If you don't find your answer here, please feel free to contact us.

What is FlickCase?

FlickCase is a mobile application built with Compose Multiplatform that helps you discover new movies and TV shows, explore them by genre, and find detailed information.

How do I search for movies and TV shows?

You can use the search bar located at the top of the Search screen. Simply type in the title of the movie or TV show you are looking for.

How do I browse by genre?

On the Home screen, you'll find a "Browse by Genre" section. Tap on a genre to see a list of movies and TV shows within that category. You can also find a comprehensive list of all genres on the dedicated "Genres" screen in the bottom navigation.

Where does the movie and TV show information come from?

FlickCase utilizes the TMDb API to provide you with up-to-date and accurate information about movies and TV shows.

Is FlickCase free to use?

Yes, FlickCase is currently free to use.
